Singapore Math: An Overview

Recognized as the world's top math curriculum, Singapore Math has propelled Singaporean students to the #1 spot in global assessments like TIMSS and PISA. Developed in the 1980s by Dr. Kho Tek Hong and a team of experts, this curriculum focuses on deep understanding, problem-solving, and mastery of key concepts.

Key Features

  • Concrete-Pictorial-Abstract (CPA): Students progress from hands-on tools (e.g., cubes) to visual models (e.g., bar diagrams) to abstract equations.
  • Mastery Approach: Fewer topics are taught in greater depth, ensuring students master each concept before moving on.
  • Problem-Solving Focus: Bar modeling and mental math strategies empower students to tackle complex word problems with ease.
  • Rigorous Progression: Builds on prior knowledge, creating a strong foundation from primary grades onward.

Sample Curriculum Progression

Grade Level Key Topics
Grade 1 Number bonds, addition/subtraction within 20, place value basics
Grade 3 Multiplication/division, fractions, bar modeling for word problems
